GOT QUESTIONS?

Frequently Asked QuestionS

Find answers about Tonemaki's model, tools, and processes...

What exactly is Tonemaki™?

Alright, let’s break it down. If you’re a brand seeking to stand out with comprehensive visual solutions, Tonemaki™ is your go-to bespoke productized design service. Our model lets you skip the hiring hassle as we craft unique art, design, and tech experiences across 2D, 3D, Motion, and Web. Think of us as your personal sushi chef for design, creating a distinctive identity to drive sustained growth.

Who are the designers?

Tonemaki™ it’s led by Tone Segurado, an award-winning generalist fullstack designer, animator, and illustrator. Check out his website here to see who you’re working with.

Are you always open to new clients?

It depends on our capacity. If you spot 'Sold Out,' it means we're at full tilt, focusing exclusively on our current clients. Quality attention is key—Generalists and Full-Stackers at our rates are rare, and we want to ensure every client gets the care they deserve. We recommend checking back or reach out Tone directly for notifications when slots reopen.

How do I get started with Tonemaki™?

Easy. Simply click “Get Started” to your plan, or Schedule a Free call. You’ll get your Private Trello board link within 24 hours, and boom, you're in business. Start requesting and receiving designs just like that.

How easy Is It to request a design?

Incredibly. Submit a Request in Seconds, and We’ll Handle the Rest. We use Trello to manage your design requests and deliverables. You’ll receive access to your private Trello board upon subscribing, where you can add tasks, review progress, and provide feedback. Additionally, all billing and subscription details can be managed through our secure Stripe client portal, which you can access via a one-time passcode sent to your email.

How many design requests can I make?

No limits. Pile them up in your backlog column. We’ll tackle them one at a time (or two with the Emperor plan) until they're all done.

Can you explain your workflow?

Sure, here’s how it works based on the service:
Visual ID:
Pre-Production: Briefing, Discovery
Production: Logo, Type & Color, Visual Messaging & Voice, Print & Digital Pieces
Post-Production: Brand Guidelines
Website:
Pre-Production: Briefing, Wireframe, Web Design
Production: Webflow Development, Visuals
Post-Production: Iterate, Delivery, On-Air
2D/3D Illustration:
Pre-Production: Briefing, Manual Sketch, Digital Sketch
Production: Modeling, Lights/Shadows, Texturing Materials
Post-Production: Rendering, Final Artwork
Motion Design:
Pre-Production: Briefing, Your Script, Style Frames, File Preparation
Production: Your Voice Over, Animation, Your Audio Mix
Post-Production: Final Composition, Rendering, Sign Off

What tools do you use?

We use the best out there: Maxon One, Figma, Adobe CC, Webflow, GSAP, GSG+, and more. It’s like having the best ingredients and knives in a sushi kitchen.

What if I’m not satisfied with a delivery?

We’re Not Happy Until You Are. If you don’t like it, toss it back into the “active requests” column on Trello. We’ll keep working until you’re happy. It’s like sending back a bad sushi roll – we avoid bad experiences at all costs.

Are there any additional costs?

No hidden fees for our design services. Our pricing is transparent and covers everything we create. However, some third-party costs may arise, like purchasing website domains, hosting, fonts, or music licenses. These services are not part of our offerings and would need to be paid directly to their respective providers. For example, licensing a font for a large organization could involve a significant fee, depending on the number of users. We use tools for design, but you handle third-party purchases.
